Hello! I am Smit Kapadia, a rising junior in Aerospace Engineering at Purdue University! This website features my engineering projects and photography.

I am driven by a strong desire to learn how things work (aka curiosity) and a love for processes which involve attention to detail, hardwork and creative perspectives. From learning how aircraft wings work to performing structural analyses of components, and building things, I have come to appreciate the diverse set of problems which call for varying skill-sets in Aerospace Engineering. Outside of school and my technical team, I am deeply committed to expressing the beauty of nature through my photographs, by experimenting with various compositions and lighting conditions!
