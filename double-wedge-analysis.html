Fin Double-Wedge Airfoil analysis

(Jan - July 2022)

Description

To optimize the double-wedge supersonic airfoil of my rocket team's fins for drag, I integrated compressible flow equations in MATLAB to calculate the pressure ratio across oblique and expansion shocks. Drag can be derived from these pressure ratios. Additionally, I modeled a 2D double-wedge airfoil in CFD using Ansys FLUENT to determine the drag coefficient, and validate with hand calculations.
