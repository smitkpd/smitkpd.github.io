Fin Double-Wedge Airfoil analysis

To determine the optimal double-wedge airfoil shape for my rocket team's fins, I wrote a script to calculate drag across a supersonic double-wedge airfoil. I used the compressible flow equations for oblique and expansion shocks, to calculate the pressure ratio at each station. Drag can be derived from these pressure ratios. Additionally, I modeled a 2D double-wedge airfoil in CFD using Ansys FLUENT to determine the drag coefficient, and validate with hand calculations.
